When We Work Together

Here's What We Work On

01.

Building your program from the ground up

We start with a detailed intake form and a kick-off call — covering your goals, schedule, equipment, injury history, and current habits. Nothing is assumed. Your program is built completely from scratch based on who you are and where you want to go.

02.

Training you can actually execute — correctly

Your custom training program comes with video demonstrations for every exercise so there's no guesswork. Send me videos of your lifts anytime and I'll respond with written or recorded video feedback on your form, tempo, and intensity — because since I can't see you in person, this is how we keep you training safely and effectively.

03.

Progressing week to week so you never plateau

Your program isn't static. I track your progress and apply gradual progressive overload week to week — adjusting weights, volume, and structure based on how your body is responding. Your cardio and daily step count protocol is updated alongside this to support your overall goals.

04.

Aligning your nutrition to your goals and your life

We start by assessing your current eating habits before setting any targets. From there you'll receive custom macro goals or a meal plan — whichever suits you best — along with meal ideas, grocery lists, and educational resources on tracking, eating out, and navigating your social life without derailing your progress.

05.

Learning to understand your body through tracking

Tracking your food isn't forever — but it's one of the most powerful tools for understanding what your body actually needs. We use it to build awareness and give you a real foundation, so that when you transition to a more intuitive approach, you're doing it with knowledge and confidence rather than guesswork.

06.

Checking in and adjusting every single week

Every week we review your training and nutrition — what's working, what needs to change, and what we're focusing on next. You'll receive in-depth written or video feedback, and 1:1 calls are available as needed — you're never left to figure it out on your own.

07.

Building habits that change how you live — for good

The goal was never just to hit a number on the scale. It's to build a lifestyle where taking care of yourself feels natural — where the way you train, eat, and think about your body becomes part of who you are. That's what lasting results actually look like.
